# BetrFood - Feature Specification

## Overview
BetrFood is a social media platform dedicated to food content, combining Instagram/TikTok-style posts with AI-powered cooking assistance and personalized dietary management.

---

## 1. Content Posting & Feed

### Post Creation
- **Photo Posts**: Share single or multiple food photos with captions
- **Video Posts**: Short-form video content (similar to TikTok/Instagram Reels)
- **Recipe Posts**: Structured posts with ingredients, instructions, and timing
- **Meal Posts**: Tag meals with metadata (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack)
- **Post Editing**: Edit captions, tags, and metadata after publishing
- **Draft Saving**: Save posts as drafts before publishing

### Post Interactions
- **Like/Unlike**: React to posts
- **Comments**: Threaded commenting system
- **Share**: Share posts to other platforms or within BetrFood
- **Save**: Bookmark posts for later viewing
- **Report**: Flag inappropriate content

### Feed Types
- **Home Feed**: Personalized algorithmic feed based on interests and following
- **Following Feed**: Chronological feed from followed accounts
- **Explore Feed**: Discover new content and trending posts
- **Category Feeds**: Browse by cuisine type, dietary preference, meal type

---

## 2. Search & Discovery

### Account Search
- Search users by username, name, or bio
- Filter by verified accounts, chefs, nutritionists
- View account preview cards with follower count and sample posts

### Post Search
- Search posts by keywords, hashtags, or ingredients
- Advanced filters:
  - Dietary restrictions (vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, etc.)
  - Cuisine type (Italian, Mexican, Asian, etc.)
  - Meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack)
  - Cooking time and difficulty
  - Ingredient inclusion/exclusion

### Trending
- Trending hashtags
- Trending recipes
- Viral food content

---

## 3. Social Features

### Following System
- **Follow/Unfollow**: Connect with other users
- **Followers List**: View who follows you with profile previews
- **Following List**: View accounts you follow
- **Follow Suggestions**: AI-powered recommendations based on interests

### Engagement Analytics
- **Post Analytics**: View who liked, commented, and saved your posts
- **Follower Analytics**: Track follower growth over time
- **Engagement Metrics**: Likes, comments, shares, saves per post

### Notifications
- New followers
- Likes and comments on your posts
- Mentions and tags

---

## 4. AI Chatbot for Food

### Food Information
- **Ingredient Information**: Nutritional facts, origins, seasonality
- **Cooking Techniques**: How-to guides and tips
- **Flavor Profiles**: Ingredient pairing suggestions
- **Substitutions**: Alternative ingredients for dietary needs
- **Storage Tips**: How to store and preserve ingredients

### Conversational Features
- Natural language queries about food
- Recipe suggestions based on available ingredients
- Cooking problem-solving (e.g., "How do I fix oversalted soup?")
- Cultural and historical food context
- Dietary and nutritional guidance

### Integration
- Accessible from any post or recipe
- Context-aware responses based on current content
- Save chat history for reference

---

## 5. Profile Management

### User Profile
- **Profile Picture & Bio**: Customize your presence
- **Display Name & Username**: Personal branding
- **Verification Badge**: For chefs, nutritionists, influencers
- **Profile Stats**: Post count, followers, following
- **Post Grid**: Visual gallery of all posts
- **Saved Collections**: Organized bookmarks of favorite posts

### Dietary Preferences & Health
- **Food Preferences**:
  - Favorite cuisines
  - Preferred cooking methods
  - Flavor preferences (spicy, sweet, savory, etc.)

- **Allergies & Intolerances**:
  - Common allergens (nuts, dairy, shellfish, etc.)
  - Specific ingredient allergies
  - Severity levels

- **Dietary Restrictions**:
  - Vegetarian, Vegan, Pescatarian
  - Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Nut-free
  - Religious restrictions (Halal, Kosher)
  - Medical diets (Low sodium, diabetic-friendly, etc.)

- **Nutritional Goals**:
  - Calorie targets
  - Macro nutrient preferences
  - Health objectives (weight loss, muscle gain, etc.)

### Privacy Settings
- Profile visibility (public, private, friends-only)
- Content sharing preferences
- Dietary information visibility
- Block and mute users

## 6. Additional Features

### Collaboration
- **Cooking Together**: Invite followers to cook the same recipe
- **Recipe Collections**: Create and share curated recipe collections

### Monetization (Future)
