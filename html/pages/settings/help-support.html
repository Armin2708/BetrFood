Frequently Asked Questions
How do I update my dietary restrictions?
Go to Profile > Settings > Health Info. Here you can add or update your allergies, dietary preferences, and nutritional goals. This helps the AI give you better recommendations.
How does the AI chat work?
The AI chat analyzes food posts and provides personalized recipe suggestions, ingredient substitutions, and cooking tips based on your dietary preferences. Free users get 10 prompts per day, premium users get unlimited.
Can I delete my account?
Yes. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and will remove all your data including posts, followers, and chat history.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Tap the three dots on any post or comment and select "Report". Choose a reason and our moderation team will review it within 24 hours.
What's included in Premium?
Premium includes unlimited AI prompts, advanced recipe analytics, no ads, early access to new features, and a verified badge on your profile. It's $9.99/month or $79.99/year.
