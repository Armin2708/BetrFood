Can you give me the full recipe for this carbonara?
Based on Chef Marcus's post, here's a classic carbonara recipe: Ingredients: - 400g spaghetti - 200g guanciale or pancetta - 4 large egg yolks - 100g Pecorino Romano, freshly grated - Black pepper - Salt for pasta water Instructions: 1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water 2. Crisp the guanciale in a pan 3. Mix egg yolks with grated cheese 4. Remove pan from heat 5. Add drained pasta to the pan 6. Add egg mixture and toss quickly 7. Season with black pepper The key tip from the post: take the pan off heat before adding eggs to avoid scrambling!
What if I can't find Pecorino Romano?
Great question! While Pecorino Romano is traditional, you can substitute with Parmigiano-Reggiano (Parmesan). Some cooks even use a 50/50 blend. Pecorino is sharper and saltier, while Parmesan is milder and nuttier. Both work beautifully in carbonara!
