Recipe Details

Recipe Info
Prep Time
15min
Cook Time
25min
Servings
4
Difficulty
Medium
Ingredients
2 cups all-purpose flour
3 large eggs
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp salt
Instructions
1
Pour flour onto a clean work surface and make a well in the center. Crack eggs into the well and add olive oil and salt.
2
Using a fork, gently beat the eggs and begin to incorporate the flour from the sides of the well.
3
Knead the dough for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
