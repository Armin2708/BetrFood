# BetrFood

A social media platform dedicated to food lovers, combining Instagram/TikTok-style content sharing with community discussions, AI-powered assistance, and intelligent meal planning.

## Overview

BetrFood is a comprehensive mobile food social platform that brings together food enthusiasts, home cooks, professional chefs, and nutritionists. Share your culinary creations, discover new recipes, engage in food discussions, and let AI help you plan your meals based on your dietary preferences and health goals.

## Key Features

### Social Content Platform
- **Rich Media Sharing**: Post photos, videos, and structured recipes with your food creations
- **Interactive Feed**: Personalized algorithmic feed and chronological following feed
- **Engagement**: Like, comment, share, and save favorite posts
- **Stories**: Share temporary content that disappears after 24 hours
- **Social Connection**: Follow other users, discover new creators, and build your food community

### Community Discussions
- **Topic-Based Communities**: Join communities organized by cuisine types, dietary preferences, cooking techniques, and local food scenes
- **Reddit-Style Discussions**: Create text posts with upvoting/downvoting, threaded comments, and community moderation
- **Flair System**: Tag posts with relevant categories for better organization
- **Community Management**: Create and moderate your own food communities

### AI-Powered Chatbot
- **Food Intelligence**: Ask questions about ingredients, cooking techniques, flavor profiles, and substitutions
- **Recipe Assistance**: Get recipe suggestions based on available ingredients and problem-solving help
- **Context-Aware**: Access AI insights directly from any post or recipe
- **Conversational**: Natural language queries with saved chat history

### Smart Meal Planning
- **One-Click Plans**: Generate meal plans from any post or combine multiple posts
- **Smart Shopping Lists**: Automatic ingredient aggregation with store section organization
- **Nutritional Tracking**: Daily calorie and macro nutrient breakdowns aligned with your goals
- **Prep Instructions**: Step-by-step cooking schedules with batch cooking suggestions
- **Cooking Mode**: Full-screen, hands-free recipe viewing with voice control

### Personalized Profile & Health
- **Dietary Preferences**: Set favorite cuisines, cooking methods, and flavor preferences
- **Allergy Management**: Track food allergies and intolerances with severity levels
- **Dietary Restrictions**: Support for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, and medical diets
- **Nutritional Goals**: Set calorie targets, macro preferences, and health objectives
- **Content Curation**: Save posts to collections and organize your favorite recipes

### Advanced Search & Discovery
- **Multi-Category Search**: Find posts, accounts, recipes, and communities
- **Advanced Filters**: Filter by dietary restrictions, cuisine type, meal type, cooking time, and difficulty
- **Trending Content**: Discover viral posts, trending recipes, and rising creators
- **Smart Recommendations**: AI-powered suggestions based on your interests and cooking history

## App Structure

### Navigation
The app features a bottom navigation bar with 5 main sections:
- **Home**: Personalized feed of food content
- **Search/Explore**: Discover new content and users
- **Create**: Post new content (center action button)
- **Communities**: Browse and engage in topic-based discussions
- **Profile**: Your profile, settings, and meal plans

### Screen Count
**77 unique UI pages** organized in three development phases:

**Phase 1 - MVP** (Essential): Authentication, core feed, basic posting, search, profiles, AI chat, and meal planning basics

**Phase 2 - Core Features** (Enhanced): Full post creation workflow, dietary management, communities, shopping lists, and settings

**Phase 3 - Advanced** (Premium): Stories, direct messages, analytics, collections, and creator tools

## User Flows

### Getting Started
1. Splash screen → Welcome → Sign Up/Login
2. Onboarding slides introducing key features
3. Dietary profile setup (optional, can skip)
4. Interest selection and follow suggestions
5. Arrive at personalized home feed

### Content Sharing
1. Tap create button (+)
2. Take photo/video or select from gallery
3. Apply filters and edit
4. Add caption, hashtags, location, and tags
5. For recipes: add ingredients, instructions, and timing
6. Publish or save as draft

### Meal Planning Journey
1. Browse feed and find interesting recipe post
2. Tap "Create Meal Plan" button
3. Select meal type, date, and serving size
4. AI generates meal plan with nutritional info
5. Access auto-generated shopping list
6. Follow prep instructions
7. Use cooking mode for hands-free guidance

### Community Engagement
1. Navigate to Communities tab
2. Browse or search for topics of interest
3. Join communities
4. Create discussion posts or engage with existing content
5. Upvote helpful posts and participate in threaded discussions

## Why BetrFood?

BetrFood stands out by combining the visual appeal of Instagram, the engaging short-form video of TikTok, the community discussions of Reddit, and the practical utility of meal planning apps - all powered by AI to make cooking and meal management effortless and enjoyable.

Whether you're looking for dinner inspiration, want to share your culinary masterpiece, need help planning a week of meals around your dietary needs, or simply want to connect with fellow food lovers, BetrFood is your all-in-one food social platform.
